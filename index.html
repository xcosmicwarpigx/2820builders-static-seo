Our Services

Building Success, Together

At 28:20 Builders, we deliver exceptional results through clear communication, meticulous planning, and genuine collaboration. Our approach ensures every project, from residential homes to commercial spaces, is completed on time and on budget.

Residential Construction

We bring your dream home to life with meticulous attention to detail and open communication throughout the process. From custom designs to energy-efficient solutions, we create spaces that reflect your unique vision and lifestyle.

Residential Remodeling

Transform your existing space into the home you’ve always wanted, balancing aesthetics with functionality. Our team expertly handles everything from single-room updates to whole-house renovations, minimizing disruption while maximizing your home’s potential.

Commercial Build-outs

We specialize in tenant interiors for office, retail, and industrial build-outs, as well as white box demolition. Our tailored commercial services deliver functional, attractive spaces on time and on budget, benefiting both tenants and building owners. Our expertise supports your business goals and helps secure long-term leases.

    Got More Questions?

    How do I get started?

    To get started, simply fill out our contact form or give us a call. Our team will guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

    What services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of services including residential construction, commercial build-outs, remodeling, and more. 

    How long does a project usually take?

    The duration of a project depends on various factors such as its complexity and size. Our team will provide you with a timeline during the consultation phase.

    What is your pricing structure?

    Our pricing structure is tailored to each project and takes into account factors such as materials, labor, and scope of work. Contact us for a personalized quote.

    How do you work with home buyers?

    We partner with real estate agents to help home buyers transform potential properties into their dream homes. If you’ve found a house in an ideal location but it doesn’t quite meet your needs, we can help. We provide vision, consulting and quick turnaround budgets for remodeling, allowing you to understand the costs and possibilities of customizing the home to your preferences.
